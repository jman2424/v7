# Offer & CTA Templates

## Product Offer (Single)
- “**{item_name}** — **{price} {unit_label}**. Want me to reserve it at **{branch_name}**?”

## Bundle for People Count
- “**{bundle_name}** serves **{people_count}** for **{total_price}**. Pickup or delivery?”

## Cross-Sell / Association
- “Most people add **{associated_item_name}** with that. Want to include it?”

## Substitution (Out-of-Stock)
- “**{item_name}** is out of stock. Prefer **{alt_item_name}** for **{alt_price}**?”

## Delivery CTA
- “Delivery to **{postcode}** is **{fee}** (min **{min_order}**). Want me to start your order?”

## Pickup CTA
- “I can have it ready at **{branch_name}**. What time suits you?”

## Category Shortlist (Max 2)
- “Popular in **{category_name}**: **{item_a}** or **{item_b}**. Which one?”

## Discount/Promo (If Policy Allows)
- “There’s a **{promo_name}** on **{item_or_category}**. Want the details?”

## Rules
- Keep to one CTA.
- Prefer 1–2 explicit choices when offering lists.
- Prices and availability must be verified from catalog/policy before proposing.
