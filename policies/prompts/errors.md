# Error & Fallback Templates

## Generic Failure (Unknown)
- “Something went wrong on my side. Want me to try again or pass this to a colleague?”

## Tool Timeout / External Service
- “I couldn’t reach that service just now. Shall I retry or take your number?”

## Missing Data (Grounding)
- “Not in my info. I can check with the team — want me to text you when I have it?”

## Invalid Input
- “I didn’t catch that. Could you share **{expected_input_example}**?”

## Policy Conflict
- “That doesn’t match our current policy. I can double-check with a colleague — should I?”

## Rate-Limit / Too Many Requests
- “I’m getting a lot at once. Give me a moment, or I can text you when it’s ready.”

## Escalation Triggered
- “I’m handing this to a colleague now so they can sort it quickly. What’s the best number to reach you on?”
