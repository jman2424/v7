# Clarifier Templates

> Use exactly **one** clarifier when confidence < threshold or required entity is missing.
> Keep to **one sentence** unless otherwise noted. No emojis.

## Missing Postcode (Delivery/Nearest Branch)
- “What’s your postcode? I’ll check delivery and the nearest branch.”

## Ambiguous Item (Two Options Max)
- “Do you mean **{item_a}** or **{item_b}**?”

## Missing Size/Weight/Variant
- “Which size do you want: **{option_a}** or **{option_b}**?”

## Uncovered Area (Offer Alternative)
- “We mainly cover **{prefixes_joined}**. Are you close to **{nearest_branch_name}**?”

## Time Specifics
- “For **{date_or_day}**, do you want **pickup** or **delivery**?”

## Availability Unclear
- “Do you want me to **check stock** for **{item_name}** at **{branch_name}**?”

## Budget/Quantity
- “Roughly how many people is this for — **{people_a}** or **{people_b}**?”

## Contact Capture (When Needed)
- “What’s the best **phone number** so we can confirm quickly?”

## Rules
- Never ask more than one clarifier per turn.
- Prefer offering **two explicit choices** when possible.
- If second clarifier would be needed, escalate to human.
